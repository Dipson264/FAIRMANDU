About Fairmandu

A platform for tourist to seek for fair tourism

Our Agenda

The application has its main purpose, and this is to create a level playing ground in pricing. Allowing tourists access of the current price information can help make sure tourists understand what they should expect and what would be considered as too much. promote Nepal.


Furthermore, businesses with such prices will receive positive feedback, whereas those who play the scam game will have more challenges and probably lose customers. It will achieve a win-win scenario: it will benefit the tourists by making expenses cheaper, and it will enable the local honest businessman who are genuine players to be recognized.


This might in turn reduce the scams in Thamel over time and thus track the positive sentiments of the tourists making them stay and promote Nepal.
